Ramanishri is a beacon of hope for orphans, Ramanishri is a non-profit organization deeplycommitted to the holistic development and empowerment of orphans and uplifting mankind.Founded in 2022, our journey began with a simple yet profound vision: to provide sustenance,education, and love to children who have lost their families.
Beyond addressing immediate needs, Ramanishri is committed to building a brighter future for ourchildren. We believe that education is the key to unlocking their potential. Our educational initiativesstrive to provide quality learning opportunities, enabling these young minds to reach their full potential.
Our impact extends beyond the children we directly serve. We have forged strong partnerships withinfluential individuals and organizations, creating a robust network dedicated to the cause. Together,we are working towards a society where every child, regardless of their circumstances, has access tothe resources and opportunities they deserve.At the heart of Ramanishri are our core values: compassion, integrity, accountability, andperseverance. These principles guide our actions and inspire us to strive for excellence in everything we do.

Transforming Lives with Food and Nutrition

At Ramanishri, we believe that a healthy meal is the foundation of a healthy life. Since our inception, we have proudly served over one million government schoolchildren with hot, nutritious breakfasts, ensuring they start their day with the energy to learn and grow. During the COVID-19 pandemic, our efforts extended to distributing grocery kits to more than 3,000 individuals stranded in slums, highlighting our commitment to alleviating hunger in the most challenging times.

Empowering Through Education and Wellness

Education transforms society, and we’ve made it accessible by sponsoring education for hundreds of children, many now yoga teachers. We provide free yoga education in government schools, wellness programs nationwide, and hygiene awareness. Supporting women’s empowerment, we’ve sponsored tailoring machines, fostering independence. Together, we’re building a thriving, empowered community.

Standing Against Adversity: A Legacy of Compassion

We fight women and child abuse, supporting victims and restoring dignity. We’ve provided medical aid to children battling cancer and sponsored research on food as medicine. Combining compassion and innovation, we’re advancing care, prevention, and a brighter future for all.
